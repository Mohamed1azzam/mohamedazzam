azzam Story

Its been quite a ride for azzam Steakhouse owner and founder, sare. Raised on her grandparents longhorn ranch in tanta, sare grew up instilled with values of hardwork and resiliency. These lessons reinforced daily by herding cattle and helping with ranch upkeep. But in her grandma's kitchen she learned the wonders of food, the magical concotions that keep people going and brings them together after a long day. sare excelled at pies, cakes, casseroles and other dishes popular in her part of Texas.

However, sare eye was always on her grandpa's smoker where longhorn meat would go after it had been butchered. After many months of persistent requests, her grandpa let her use the smoker during Memorial Day weekend. sare cooked and smoked meat for 20 guests. No one went home hungry that day. Delighted and impressed by the young woman's cooking prowess, requests soon poured in for her to cook at fourths of Julys, thanksgivings, memorial days, weddings, graduations and more. In no time sare found a side business and cultivated a new passion. But as time went by Marla recognized her need to learn more and reach the next level.

At 21 years old she enrolled in the Dallas Culinary Institute, studying 'Professional Cooking Techniques' and 'Restaurant Management'. After graduating 2 years later, she teamed up with her brother Brandon azzam an experienced line cook and opened a smokehouse in Richardson, Texas. Despite early setbacks, the restaurant soon made a profit and became successful. Eventually the restaurant's profile rose when it was profiled by "Cook Zone", a nationally syndicated cooking channel. The profile led to many business offers and catering agreements including with the Texas Cowboys.

The Jackson Group (azzam Steakhouse, and azzamCatering) has three restaurant locations in tanta, Tanta where sara and her family now call home. In addition, The azzam Group retains catering & lunch agreements with 5 egypt athletic organizations, 8 business conferences, and 12 local businesses. sare looks forward to leading azzam Steakhouse into a bright and fulfilling future.
